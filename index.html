About

Mijndert works at Rhinofly. When he’s not working his ass off, he’s probably listening to music, having a beer with friends or geeking out on one of his projects. He’d worked on some pretty cool stuff like Next Best Band and he also helped out OK GO launch Foodzy. His biggest dream is to move to London one day.

Other than being a great guy he’s also more than willing to help out startups achieve their goals by lending them his expertise. He’s pretty good with high performance IT infrastructure to make any web app go woosh.
